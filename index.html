About Me

In a heuristically-generated, algorithmically-driven world, creativity is the highest-value attribute.

I create software solutions for business. “Solution” might imply a problem to be overcome. However, I frame it more optimistically — as an opportunity to be realized.

Opportunity in business means a chance to increase revenue (i.e., sales), or maximize profit by cost reduction.

The first can be achieved by leveraging technology to make high-frequency transactions frictionless, with an unrivaled user experience. The second, by executing high-consequence decisions faster, with lower input of time, labor or other finite resources.

Please browse my featured work to launch demo apps exemplifying prior work; snippets of solutions created for clients. You can view the source for these and other projects at Github.
